Challenge #3: Violence Against Women

  • 4,200+ cases of rape and sexual assault in first 6 months of 2025
  • 650 incidents of gang rape documented
  • 35% increase from 2024
  • Many cases go unreported due to social stigma
  • Inadequate support services for survivors

Challenge #4: Rohingya Refugee Crisis

  • 1 million refugees in Cox's Bazar camps
  • World's largest refugee settlement with severe overcrowding
  • New arrivals since Aug 2024 straining limited resources
  • Inadequate healthcare, education, and livelihood opportunities
  • Rising tensions between refugee and host communities

Challenge #5: Economic Collapse

  • GDP down to 2.3% (from 6.1% in 2023)
  • Inflation at 12%, food inflation at 16%
  • Youth unemployment surged to 28%
  • Currency depreciation affecting imports
  • Foreign investment declining due to instability

Infrastructure Vulnerability

  • Two-thirds of Bangladesh less than 15 feet above sea level
  • Ranked 9th most climate-vulnerable country globally
  • 700,000 displaced annually by natural disasters
  • Weak early warning systems
  • Inadequate emergency shelters and evacuation routes
