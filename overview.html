Bangladesh Crisis Overview 2024-2025

Understanding the Current Crisis

Critical Situation

Bangladesh faces its deepest crisis in over a decade with multiple simultaneous challenges: political instability, law and order breakdown, climate disasters, and economic decline affecting millions of citizens nationwide.

Vulnerability Factors

  • Infrastructure: Two-thirds of Bangladesh less than 15 feet above sea level
  • Climate Risk: Ranked 9th most climate-vulnerable country
  • Displacement: 700,000 displaced annually by natural disasters
  • Refugee Crisis: 1 million refugees in Cox's Bazar camps (world's largest settlement)
  • Institutional Weakness: Political interference in law enforcement and governance
