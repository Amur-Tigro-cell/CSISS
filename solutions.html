Solutions for Security & Violence

Anonymous Reporting System

Citizens can report crimes, mob violence, and communal attacks without fear of retaliation. Encrypted submissions protect whistleblowers and ensure evidence preservation.

Video Evidence Upload

Direct upload of incident videos/photos to authorities, bypassing social media circulation that creates impunity culture. Secured evidence chain for prosecution.

SOS Panic Button

One-touch emergency alert broadcasts location to police, family, and nearby community volunteers instantly. Provides rapid response to violence in progress.

Minority Community Protection

Dedicated hotline and rapid response for attacks on religious minorities with direct connection to special protection units. Proactive monitoring of vulnerable areas.

Women's Safety Features

Safe route navigation, trusted contact emergency sharing, and women-only community watch networks. 24/7 support hotline specifically for women in crisis.

Mob Violence Prevention

AI-powered crowd monitoring alerts authorities to potential mob formations and automatically dispatches preventive patrols before violence occurs.

Solutions for Climate Disasters

Cyclone Early Warning

Integration with Bangladesh Meteorological Department for 48-72 hour advance cyclone warnings with evacuation route guidance and shelter pre-positioning.

Flood Alert System

Real-time water level monitoring from rivers and forecasting models predict flooding 24-48 hours in advance, enabling preventive action.

Shelter Location Mapping

Interactive map showing all 79 multi-purpose flood shelters, cyclone shelters, and safe zones with capacity information and real-time availability.

Disaster Preparedness Training

In-app tutorials and drills for cyclone, flood, heatwave, and earthquake response with community coordination protocols.

Resource Distribution Tracking

Platform for NGOs and government to coordinate relief supplies, avoid duplication, and reach underserved areas efficiently.
